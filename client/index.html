How to Play Equity Sprint

🎯 Objective

Reach $1,000,000 net worth first, or have the highest net worth after 10 years.

🔄 Each Year

The wheel is spun once → year summary shown → each player takes 2 actions in turn order.

🏠 Properties

Buy to earn rent. Renovate to boost rent & value. Develop regional properties. Release equity to free up cash. Sell at market value.

📊 Key Stats

Net Worth = Cash + (Value − Debt). Serviceability = (Salary + Rent) × 0.7 − Repayments — must stay positive to buy.

🎡 Wheel Events

Economic Event: affects all. Market Change: shifts rules for 1–2 years. Influence Card: secret weapon. Chance: personal bonus.

💳 Influence Cards

Held privately (up to 3). Play anytime — free action. Target opponents, their properties, or the market.
